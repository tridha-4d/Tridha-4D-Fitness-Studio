I mainly treat pain and paralyasis cases like knee pain, slip disc, frozen shoulder, tennis elbow, hemiplegia, paraplegia, facial paralysis etc.
The methods used are...

ELECTROACUPUNCTURE

Tiny needles pricked at the acupuncture points are stimulated by an electrostimulator.

Only sterile disposable needles are used.

MOXIBUSTION

Dry leaves of moxa (Artemisia vulgaris) are used to heat and stimulate the points.

It is very effective in disordres caused or aggravated by cold eg. asthma, rhinitis, diarrhoea, arthritis etc.

CUPPING/ FIRE CUPPING/ BEKAM THERAPY

Acupuncture points are bled and blood is cupped out by applying negative pressure with the help of a cup.

It is very useful in spondylosis, arthritis, backache, diarrhoea etc.

PLUM BLOSSOM THERAPY

Acupuncture points and meridians are tapped with hammer having bunch of needles.

Useful for leucoderma, alopecia, and for maintainance of acupuncture treatment as patient can stimulate his/her own points.
